Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I restore my Pro purchase?
A: Tap on any locked feature in the main menu to open the Paywall, then tap the "Restore Purchases" button at the bottom.
Q: The camera doesn't track my movements well.
A: Make sure you are playing in a well-lit room and stand at least 2 meters (6 feet) away from the device so your full body is visible.
